Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the MemsRoom app (hereinafter referred to as "the App") created by ZhengLingxiao. The App is provided as a local utility tool that allows users to move selected photos and videos into the App's private directory so that they are no longer visible in the system gallery. Users may also restore those files back to the system gallery or permanently delete them.

2. Data Collection and Use

The App is designed to work entirely on your device.

  • We do not collect any personal data.
  • We do not require account registration or user login.
  • We do not upload your photos, videos, or any other data to external servers.
  • We do not back up your data to any cloud service.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

The App only processes photos and videos that you explicitly choose. Its core function is to move files locally from one folder or storage location to another private app directory on your device.

The App does not compress, encrypt, rename, or change the file extension of your photos or videos. It only moves them for local storage management.

3. Local Storage and File Handling

All selected photos and videos remain stored locally on your device. When files are moved into the App's private directory, they may no longer appear in the system gallery. You may choose to move them back to the system gallery at any time, or delete them permanently from within the App.

Please note that if you uninstall the App, clear the App's data, or reset your device to factory settings, any photos or videos stored in the App's private directory may be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

4. Permissions

The App may request access to photos and videos on your device only when necessary to let you select, move, restore, display, or delete files as part of the App's core functionality. These permissions are not used to collect personal information or transmit data externally.

5. Children’s Privacy

Since the App does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will become effective immediately after it is published.
